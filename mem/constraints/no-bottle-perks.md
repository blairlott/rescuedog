---
name: Birthday & Bottle Perks
description: Compliance — no free bottle giveaways; Mailchimp handles birthday discount codes
type: constraint
---
NEVER promise free bottles, "birthday bottle perks", or any bottle giveaways anywhere on the site. Giving away bottles is not alcohol-compliant.

Birthday rewards are handled OUTSIDE the site via Mailchimp (sends a discount code on customer birthdays). On-site copy may mention "we'll send you a little something on your birthday" but must NOT promise a specific bottle, free product, or quantified perk.

Same rule applies to pet birthdays / adopt-iversaries — soft sentiment only, no promo promises.
