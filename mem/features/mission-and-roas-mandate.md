- Reject "revenue at any ROAS" plays (volume without margin) and "ROAS at any revenue" plays (margin without scale).
- The bar is BOTH levers up. Optimizations should compound, not trade off.
- Frame internal/team decisions through this lens: "Does this raise revenue AND ROAS, or trade one for the other?"

**Why:** Blair directive 2026-05-23. This is the team mantra.
