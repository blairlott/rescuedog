---
name: No Free Gifts with Wine
description: Tied-house/ABC compliance — wine brands cannot give away merch or free items with wine purchases
type: constraint
---

Wine brands are prohibited by federal/state alcohol law (tied-house rules) from giving away free merchandise, totes, glassware, or any "free gift" tied to a wine purchase.

**Forbidden:** "Free tote at 18 bottles", "Free glass with case", "Buy wine get merch free".
**Allowed:** Discounts on wine itself (case discount, member %), shipping included on threshold (shipping is a service tied to the wine, not a separate good).

Removed the "Free tote" milestone from `FreeShippingBar` wine mode (May 2026). Do not re-add gift/merch reward tiers to wine cart progression.
